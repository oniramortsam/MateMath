disposizioni

Quante sono le funzioni iniettive tra due insiemi?
Diciamo:
  • \({\cal S}\left({\rm A}\right)\) le permutazioni di \({\rm A}\).
  • \({{\rm n}+1}!={\rm n}!\cdot\left({\rm n}+1\right)\) il fattoriale.
Che \(\left|{\cal S}\left({\rm A}\right)\right|=\left|{\rm A}\right|!\) lo vediamo dopo.
Anzitutto costruiamo: \[\left\{{\rm f}\in{\rm B}^{\rm A}\quad\left|\operatorname{Imm}\left({\rm f}\right)\right|=\left|{\rm A}\right|\right\}\leftrightarrow\left\{{\rm f}\in\left|{\rm B}\right|^{\left|{\rm n}\right|}\quad\left|\operatorname{Imm}\left({\rm f}\right)\right|=\left|{\rm A}\right|\right\}\] Dopodichè: \[\left\{{\rm f}\in{{\rm m}+1}^{{\rm n}+1}\quad\left|\operatorname{Imm}\left({\rm f}\right)\right|=\left|{\rm n}\right|\right\}\leftrightarrow\left({\rm m}+1\right)\times\left\{{\rm f}\in{\rm m}^{\rm n}\quad\left|\operatorname{Imm}\left({\rm f}\right)\right|=\left|{\rm n}\right|\right\}\] Col seguente criterio: \[\forall{\rm k}\in{\rm n}\quad\begin{cases}{\rm g}_1\left({\rm k}\right)\lt{\rm g}_0\Longrightarrow{\rm f}\left({\rm k}+1\right)={\rm g}_1\left({\rm k}\right)\\{\rm g}_1\left({\rm k}\right)\ge{\rm g}_0\Longrightarrow{\rm f}\left({\rm k}+1\right)={\rm g}_1\left({\rm k}\right)+1\end{cases}\] Da cui si ottiene: \[\left|{\rm f}\in{\rm B}^{\rm A}\quad\left|\operatorname{Imm}\left({\rm f}\right)\right|=\left|{\rm A}\right|\right|=\left(\prod_{\left|{\rm B}\right|-\left|{\rm A}\right|\lt{\rm k}\le\left|{\rm B}\right|}{\rm k}\right)=\frac{\left|{\rm B}\right|!}{\left(\left|{\rm B}\right|-\left|{\rm A}\right|\right)!}\]

combinazioni

In quanti modi posso distribuire \({\rm n}\) Wonka ad \({\rm m}\) Oompaloompa?
Diciamo: \[{\cal C}_{\rm n}\left({\rm B}\right)=\left\{{\rm f}\in{\mathbb N}^{\rm B}\quad\sum_{{\rm b}\in{\rm B}}{\rm f}\left(\rm B\right)={\rm n}\right\}\] Dato un elemento \({\rm c}\in{\cal C}_{\left|{\rm A}\right|}\left({\rm B}\right)\): \[{\cal P}_{\rm c}\left({\rm B}\right)=\left\{{\rm f}\in{\rm B}^{\rm A}\quad\left[\forall{\rm b}\in{\rm B}{\rm f}^{-1}\left({\rm b}\right)={\rm c}\left({\rm b}\right)\right]\right\}\]
\[\left|{\cal C}_{\rm n}\left({\rm m}\right)\right|=\left|{\cal P}_{{\rm n},{\rm m}}\left(2\right)\right|\]

permutazioni

Fissiamo \({\rm f}\in{\rm B}^{\rm A}\): \[\left\{\phi\in{\cal S}\left({\rm A}\right)\quad{\rm f}\circ\phi={\rm f}\right\}\leftrightarrow\prod_{{\rm b}\in{\rm B}}{\cal S}\left({\rm f}^{-1}\left({\rm b}\right)\right)\] Da cui per passaggio al quoziente: \[\left|{\cal P}_{\rm c}\left({\rm B}\right)\right|=\frac{\left|{\rm A}\right|!}{\prod_{{\rm b}\in{\rm B}}{\rm c}\left({\rm b}\right)!}\]
Notare che: \[\forall\left({\rm a},{\rm c}\right)\in{\rm A}\times\operatorname{Imm}\left({\rm f}\right)\quad\left|{\rm f}\circ\phi\quad\phi\in{\cal S}\left({\rm A}\right)\land{\rm f}\circ\phi\left({\rm a}\right)={\rm b}\right|=\frac{\left|{\rm A}\right|!\cdot\left|{\rm B}\right|!}{\left(\left|{\rm f}^{-1}\left({\rm b}\right)\right|-1\right)!\cdot\prod_{{\rm c}\in\operatorname{Imm}\left({\rm f}\right)\setminus\left\{{\rm b}\right\}}\left|{\rm f}^{-1}\left({\rm c}\right)\right|!}\] Da cui si ricava Stifel: \[\forall{\rm c}\in{\cal C}_{\rm n}\left({\rm B}\right)\quad{\cal P}_{\rm c}\left({\rm B}\right)=\sum_{{\rm b}\in{\rm B}}{\cal P}_{{\rm c}-1_{\rm b}}\]
Notiamo che usando Stifel: \[\left(\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm m}}{\rm a}_{\rm k}\right)^{\rm n}=\sum_{{\rm c}\in{\cal C}_{\rm n}\left({\rm m}\right)}\left({\rm n}!\cdot\prod_{{\rm k}\in{\rm m}}\frac{{\rm a}_{\rm k}^{{\rm c}_{\rm k}}}{{\rm c}_{\rm k}!}\right)\] Di questo ci interessa il caso \({\rm m}=2\): \[\left({\rm a}+{\rm b}\right)^{\rm n}=\sum_{{\rm c}\in{\rm n}+1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}!}{{\rm c}!\left({\rm n}-{\rm c}\right)!}\right)\cdot{\rm a}^{\rm c}\cdot{\rm b}^{{\rm n}-{\rm c}}\]
