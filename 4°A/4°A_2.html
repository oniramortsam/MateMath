ancora le goniometriche

Si definiscono: \[\tan\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\sin\left(\alpha\right)}{\cos\left(\alpha\right)}\qquad\cot\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\cos\left(\alpha\right)}{\sin\left(\alpha\right)}\]
Seguono le formule di addizione:
  • \[\tan\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\frac{\tan\left(\alpha\right)+\tan\left(\beta\right)}{1-\tan\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tan\left(\beta\right)}\qquad\cot\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\frac{\cot\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cot\left(\beta\right)-1}{\cot\left(\beta\right)+\cot\left(\alpha\right)}\]
  • \[\tan\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\frac{\tan\left(\alpha\right)-\tan\left(\beta\right)}{1+\tan\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tan\left(\beta\right)}\qquad\cot\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\frac{\cot\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cot\left(\beta\right)+1}{\cot\left(\beta\right)-\cot\left(\alpha\right)}\]
E di dimezzamento:
  • \[\tan\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=\frac{2\cdot\tan\left(\alpha\right)}{1-\tan\left(\alpha\right)^2}\Longrightarrow\tan\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\frac{\sqrt{\tan\left(\alpha\right)^2+1}-1}{\tan\left(\alpha\right)}\]
  • \[\cot\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=\frac{\cot\left(\alpha\right)^2-1}{2\cdot\cot\left(\alpha\right)}\Longrightarrow\cot\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\cot\left(\alpha\right)-\sqrt{1+\cot\left(\alpha\right)^2}\]
Siccome entrambe sono monotone, si definiscono le inverse: \[\operatorname{arctan}:{\mathbb R}\rightarrow\left(-\pi/2,\pi/2\right)\qquad\operatorname{arccot}:{\mathbb R}\rightarrow\left(0,\pi\right)\]

le funzioni iperboliche

Si definiscono: \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\exp\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)-1}{2\cdot\exp\left(\alpha\right)}\qquad\cosh\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\exp\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)+1}{2\cdot\exp\left(\alpha\right)}\]
Seguono le formule di addizione:
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)+\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)+\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)-\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)-\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
Seguono le formule di werner:
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+\sinh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)-\sinh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\]
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)-\cosh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+\cosh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\]
Seguono le formule di prostaferesi:
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)+\sinh\left(\beta\right)=2\cdot\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)-\sinh\left(\beta\right)=2\cdot\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha\right)-\cosh\left(\beta\right)=2\cdot\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha\right)+\cosh\left(\beta\right)=2\cdot\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
Ricaviamo le formule di dimezzamento: \[\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\sqrt{\frac{\cosh\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)-1}{2}}\qquad\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\sqrt{\frac{\cosh\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)+1}{2}}\] Si definiscono le inverse:
  • \[\operatorname{arcsinh}:\left[0,\infty\right)\rightarrow\left[0,\infty\right)\qquad\operatorname{arcsinh}\left(\alpha\right)=\log\left(\alpha+\sqrt{\alpha^2+1}\right)\]
  • \[\operatorname{arccosh}:\left[1,\infty\right)\rightarrow\left[0,\infty\right)\qquad\operatorname{arccosh}\left(\alpha\right)=\log\left(\alpha+\sqrt{\alpha^2-1}\right)\]

le tangenti iperboliche

Si definiscono: \[\tanh\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha\right)}{\cosh\left(\alpha\right)}\qquad\coth\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha\right)}{\sinh\left(\alpha\right)}\]
Seguono le formule di addizione:
  • \[\tanh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\frac{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)+\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{1+\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\qquad\coth\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)+1}{\coth\left(\beta\right)+\coth\left(\alpha\right)}\]
  • \[\tanh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\frac{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)-\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{1-\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\qquad\coth\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)-1}{\coth\left(\beta\right)-\coth\left(\alpha\right)}\]
E di dimezzamento:
  • \[\tanh\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=\frac{2\cdot\tanh\left(\alpha\right)}{1+\tanh\left(\alpha\right)^2}\Longrightarrow\tan\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\frac{1-\sqrt{1-\tanh\left(\alpha\right)^2}}{\tan\left(\alpha\right)}\]
  • \[\coth\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)^2+1}{2\cdot\coth\left(\alpha\right)}\Longrightarrow\cot\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\coth\left(\alpha\right)+\sqrt{1+\coth\left(\alpha\right)^2}\]
Si definiscono le inverse:
  • \[\operatorname{arctanh}:\left(-1,1\right)\rightarrow{\mathbb R}\qquad\operatorname{arctanh}\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\log\left(1+\alpha\right)-\log\left(1-\alpha\right)}{2}\]
  • \[\operatorname{arccoth}:{\mathbb R}\setminus\left(-1,1\right)\rightarrow{\mathbb R}\setminus1\qquad\operatorname{arccoth}\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\log\left(\alpha+1\right)-\log\left(\alpha-1\right)}{2}\]
