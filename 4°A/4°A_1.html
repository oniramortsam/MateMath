l'esponenziale e nepero

Siccome: \[\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm x}^{{\rm k}+1}}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!}\le\sum_{{\rm k}\lt{\rm x}+1}\frac{{\rm x}^{{\rm k}+1}}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!}+\frac{{\rm x}^{{\rm x}+1}}{2\cdot\left({\rm x}+2\right)^{\rm x}}\] Possiamo definire: \[\exp\left({\rm x}\right)=\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\mathbb N}}\frac{{\rm x}^{\rm k}}{{\rm k}!}=\sup_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}}\left(\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm x}^{\rm k}}{{\rm k}!}\right)\]
Consideriamo che, per \({\rm x}\ge0\), si può dimostrare: \[\lim_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}=\exp\left({\rm x}\right)\]
\[\begin{split}{\rm m}\ge{\rm n}+{\rm n}\cdot\sqrt[{\rm n}]\frac{{\rm n}-{\rm 1}}{\rm n}\Longrightarrow\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}-1&=\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm n}!}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!\left({\rm n}-{\rm k}-1\right)!}\cdot\left(\frac{\rm x}{\rm n}\right)^{{\rm k}+1}\\ &\le\left(\frac{{\rm n}-1}{\rm n}\right)\cdot\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm x}^{{\rm k}+1}}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!}\\ &\le\left(\frac{{\rm m}-{\rm n}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}\cdot\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm x}^{{\rm k}+1}}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!}\\ &\le\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm m}!}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!\left({\rm m}-{\rm k}-1\right)!}\cdot\left(\frac{\rm x}{\rm m}\right)^{{\rm k}+1}\\ &\le\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm m}}\frac{{\rm m}!}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!\left({\rm m}-{\rm k}-1\right)!}\cdot\left(\frac{\rm x}{{\rm m}}\right)^{{\rm k}+1}=\left(\frac{{\rm n}^2+{\rm x}}{\rm m}\right)^{\rm m}-1\end{split}\] Abbiamo: \[\operatorname{limsup}_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}\le\exp\left({\rm x}\right)\le\operatorname{liminf}_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}\Longrightarrow\lim_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}=\exp\left({\rm x}\right)\] come volevasi.
Considerando che: \[\forall\left({\rm n},{\rm m}\right)\in\left({\mathbb N}\setminus1\right)\times\left({\mathbb N}\setminus1\right)\quad{\rm x}\cdot{\rm m}\ge{\rm n}\Longleftrightarrow\left(\frac{{\rm m}+1}{\rm m}\right)^{{\rm m}\cdot{\rm x}}\le\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}\] Abbiamo allo stesso modo: \[\left(\lim_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+1}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}\right)^{\rm x}=\lim_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}=\exp\left({\rm x}\right)\]

le funzioni iperboliche

Si definiscono: \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\exp\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)-1}{2\cdot\exp\left(\alpha\right)}\qquad\cosh\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\exp\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)+1}{2\cdot\exp\left(\alpha\right)}\]
Seguono le formule di addizione:
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)+\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)+\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)-\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)-\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
Ricaviamo le formule di dimezzamento: \[\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\sqrt{\frac{\cosh\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)-1}{2}}\qquad\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\sqrt{\frac{\cosh\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)+1}{2}}\]
Seguono le formule di werner e di prostaferesi:
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+\sinh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\qquad\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha\right)+\sinh\left(\beta\right)}{2}=\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)-\sinh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\qquad\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha\right)-\sinh\left(\beta\right)}{2}=\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)-\cosh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\qquad\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha\right)-\cosh\left(\beta\right)}{2}=\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+\cosh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\qquad\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha\right)+\cosh\left(\beta\right)}{2}=\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]

le tangenti iperboliche

Si definiscono: \[\tanh\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha\right)}{\cosh\left(\alpha\right)}\qquad\coth\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha\right)}{\sinh\left(\alpha\right)}\]
Seguono le formule di addizione:
  • \[\tanh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\frac{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)+\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{1+\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\qquad\coth\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)+1}{\coth\left(\beta\right)+\coth\left(\alpha\right)}\]
  • \[\tanh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\frac{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)-\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{1-\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\qquad\coth\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)-1}{\coth\left(\beta\right)-\coth\left(\alpha\right)}\]
E di dimezzamento:
  • \[\tanh\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=\frac{2\cdot\tanh\left(\alpha\right)}{1+\tanh\left(\alpha\right)^2}\Longrightarrow\tanh\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\frac{1-\sqrt{1-\tanh\left(\alpha\right)^2}}{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)}\]
  • \[\coth\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)^2+1}{2\cdot\coth\left(\alpha\right)}\Longrightarrow\coth\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\coth\left(\alpha\right)+\sqrt{\coth\left(\alpha\right)^2-1}\]
Osservare che: \[\cosh\left(\theta\right)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\tanh\left(\theta\right)^2}}=\frac{1+\tanh\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2}{1-\tanh\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2}\qquad\sinh\left(\theta\right)=\frac{\tanh\left(\theta\right)}{\sqrt{1-\tanh\left(\theta\right)^2}}=\frac{2\cdot\tanh\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)}{1-\tanh\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2}\] \[\cosh\left(\theta\right)=\frac{\coth\left(\theta\right)}{\sqrt{\coth\left(\theta\right)^2-1}}=\frac{\coth\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2+1}{\coth\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2-1}\qquad\sinh\left(\theta\right)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{\coth\left(\theta\right)^2-1}}=\frac{2\cdot\coth\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)}{\coth\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2-1}\]
