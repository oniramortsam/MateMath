1. funzioni trigonometriche

Consideriamo un vettore \({\rm v}\): Come posso scrivere la rotazione di \({\rm v}\) di angolo \(\theta\)?
Dati tre punti sul piano \({\rm A},{\rm B},{\rm C}\), se \(\beta=\pi/2\), chiamiamo: \[\sin\left(\alpha\right)=\cos\left(\gamma\right)=\frac{\left|{\rm C}-{\rm B}\right|}{\left|{\rm C}-{\rm A}\right|}\qquad\cos\left(\alpha\right)=\sin\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\left|{\rm B}-{\rm A}\right|}{\left|{\rm C}-{\rm A}\right|}\] Seguono \(\dots\)
I casi particolari: \[\begin{split} \sin\left(0\right)&=0\\ \sin\left(\frac{\pi}{6}\right)&=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\\ \sin\left(\frac{\pi}{4}\right)&=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\\ \sin\left(\frac{\pi}{3}\right)&=\frac{1}{2}\\ \sin\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)&=1 \end{split}\qquad\begin{split} \cos\left(0\right)&=1\\ \cos\left(\frac{\pi}{6}\right)&=\frac{1}{2}\\ \cos\left(\frac{\pi}{4}\right)&=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\\ \cos\left(\frac{\pi}{3}\right)&=\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\\ \cos\left(\frac{\pi}{2}\right)&=0 \end{split}\] E le simmetrie: \[\sin\left({\rm x}\right)=\sin\left(\pi-{\rm x}\right)=-\sin\left({\rm x}+\pi\right)=-\sin\left(-{\rm x}\right)\] \[\cos\left({\rm x}\right)=-\cos\left(\pi-{\rm x}\right)=-\cos\left({\rm x}+\pi\right)=\cos\left(-{\rm x}\right)\]
La rotazione di un angolo \(\theta\) fa corrispondere ad un vettore \({\rm v}\) il vettore: \[\cos\left(\theta\right)\cdot{\rm v}+\sin\left(\theta\right)\cdot{\cal Z}\times{\rm v}=\cos\left(\theta\right)\cdot{\rm v}-\sin\left(\theta\right)\cdot{\rm v}\times{\cal Z}\] Dove \({\cal Z}\) è il versore uscente dal piano cartesiano:
  • \({\cal Z}\times{\rm v}\) indica la rotazione di \({\rm v}\) di \(\pi/2\) in senso antiorario.
  • \({\rm v}\times{\cal Z}\) indica la rotazione di \({\rm v}\) di \(\pi/2\) in senso orario.

2. le formule goniometriche

Consideriamo un vettore \({\rm v}\):
  • Come posso scrivere la rotazione di \({\rm v}\) di angolo \(\alpha\)?
  • Come posso scrivere la rotazione di \({\rm v}\) di angolo \(\beta\)?
  • Come posso scrivere la rotazione di \({\rm v}\) di angolo \(\alpha+\beta\)?
Seguono le formule di addizione:
  • \[\sin\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\sin\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cos\left(\beta\right)+\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sin\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\sin\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\sin\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cos\left(\beta\right)-\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sin\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\cos\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cos\left(\beta\right)-\sin\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sin\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\cos\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cos\left(\beta\right)+\sin\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sin\left(\beta\right)\]
Seguono \(\dots\)
Le formule di werner:
  • \[\sin\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cos\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\sin\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+\sin\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\]
  • \[\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sin\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\sin\left(\alpha+\beta\right)-\sin\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\]
  • \[\sin\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sin\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\cos\left(\alpha-\beta\right)-\cos\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}{2}\]
  • \[\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cos\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\cos\left(\alpha-\beta\right)+\cos\left(\alpha+\beta\right)}{2}\]
Le formule di prostaferesi:
  • \[\sin\left(\alpha\right)+\sin\left(\beta\right)=2\cdot\sin\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\cos\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\sin\left(\alpha\right)-\sin\left(\beta\right)=2\cdot\cos\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\sin\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\cos\left(\beta\right)-\cos\left(\alpha\right)=2\cdot\sin\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\sin\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\cos\left(\beta\right)+\cos\left(\alpha\right)=2\cdot\cos\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\cos\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
Siccome: \[\cos\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=2\cdot\cos\left(\alpha\right)^2-1=1-2\cdot\sin\left(\alpha\right)^2\] Ricaviamo le formule di bisezione: \[\begin{cases}\cos\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\pm\sqrt{\frac{1+\cos\left(\alpha\right)}{2}}\\\sin\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\pm\sqrt{\frac{1-\cos\left(\alpha\right)}{2}}\end{cases}\] Da cui possiamo calcolare: \[\pi=\sup_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}}\left(2^{\rm n}\cdot\sin\left(\frac{\pi}{2^{\rm n}}\right)\right)\]

3. le tangenti e cotangenti

Definiamo: \[\tan\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\sin\left(\alpha\right)}{\cos\left(\alpha\right)}\qquad\cot\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\cos\left(\alpha\right)}{\sin\left(\alpha\right)}\] Seguono \(\dots\)
Le simmetrie:
Seguono \(\dots\)
Le formule di addizione per \(\tan\): \[\begin{split} \tan\left(\alpha+\beta\right)&=\frac{\tan\left(\alpha\right)+\tan\left(\beta\right)}{1-\tan\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tan\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\tan\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cot\left(\beta\right)+1}{\cot\left(\beta\right)-\tan\left(\alpha\right)}\\ &=\frac{1+\cot\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tan\left(\beta\right)}{\cot\left(\alpha\right)-\tan\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\cot\left(\beta\right)+\cot\left(\alpha\right)}{\cot\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cot\left(\beta\right)-1}\\ \end{split}\qquad\begin{split} \tan\left(\alpha-\beta\right)&=\frac{\tan\left(\alpha\right)-\tan\left(\beta\right)}{1+\tan\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tan\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\tan\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cot\left(\beta\right)-1}{\cot\left(\beta\right)+\tan\left(\alpha\right)}\\ &=\frac{1-\cot\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tan\left(\beta\right)}{\cot\left(\alpha\right)+\tan\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\cot\left(\beta\right)-\cot\left(\alpha\right)}{\cot\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cot\left(\beta\right)+1}\\ \end{split}\] Le formule di addizione per \(\cot\): \[\begin{split} \cot\left(\alpha+\beta\right)&=\frac{1-\tan\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tan\left(\beta\right)}{\tan\left(\alpha\right)+\tan\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\cot\left(\beta\right)-\tan\left(\alpha\right)}{\tan\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cot\left(\beta\right)+1}\\ &=\frac{\cot\left(\alpha\right)-\tan\left(\beta\right)}{1+\cot\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tan\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\cot\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cot\left(\beta\right)-1}{\cot\left(\beta\right)+\cot\left(\alpha\right)}\\ \end{split}\qquad\begin{split} \cot\left(\alpha-\beta\right)&=\frac{1+\tan\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tan\left(\beta\right)}{\tan\left(\alpha\right)-\tan\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\cot\left(\beta\right)+\tan\left(\alpha\right)}{\tan\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cot\left(\beta\right)-1}\\ &=\frac{\cot\left(\alpha\right)+\tan\left(\beta\right)}{1-\cot\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tan\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\cot\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cot\left(\beta\right)+1}{\cot\left(\beta\right)-\cot\left(\alpha\right)}\\ \end{split}\]
Le formule di duplicazione: \[\tan\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=\frac{2\cdot\tan\left(\alpha\right)}{1-\tan\left(\alpha\right)^2}\qquad\cot\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=\frac{\cot\left(\alpha\right)^2-1}{2\cdot\cot\left(\alpha\right)}\]
Osservare che:
  • \[\cos\left(\theta\right)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+\tan\left(\theta\right)^2}}=\frac{1-\tan\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2}{1+\tan\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2}\qquad\sin\left(\theta\right)=\frac{\tan\left(\theta\right)}{\sqrt{1+\tan\left(\theta\right)^2}}=\frac{2\cdot\tan\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)}{1+\tan\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2}\]
  • \[\cos\left(\theta\right)=\frac{\cot\left(\theta\right)}{\sqrt{1+\cot\left(\theta\right)^2}}=\frac{\cot\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2-1}{\cot\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2+1}\qquad\sin\left(\theta\right)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+\cot\left(\theta\right)^2}}=\frac{-2\cdot\cot\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)}{\cot\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2+1}\]

4. triangoli angoli e lati

Dati tre punti sul piano \({\rm A},{\rm B},{\rm C}\) questi identificano un triangolo:
  • Chiamiamo \(\alpha\) l'angolo che ruota \({\rm B}-{\rm A}\) in \({\rm C}-{\rm A}\).
  • Chiamiamo \(\beta\) l'angolo che ruota \({\rm C}-{\rm B}\) in \({\rm A}-{\rm B}\).
  • Chiamiamo \(\gamma\) l'angolo che ruota \({\rm A}-{\rm C}\) in \({\rm B}-{\rm C}\).
Denotiamo inoltre:
  • \[{\rm a}=\left|{\rm C}-{\rm B}\right|\]
  • \[{\rm b}=\left|{\rm A}-{\rm C}\right|\]
  • \[{\rm c}=\left|{\rm C}-{\rm B}\right|\]
Come possiamo calcolare:
  • \(\frac{\rm a}{\rm b},\frac{\rm c}{\rm a},\frac{\rm b}{\rm c}\) conoscendo \(\alpha,\beta,\gamma\)?
  • \(\alpha,\beta,\gamma\) conoscendo \(\frac{\rm a}{\rm b},\frac{\rm c}{\rm a},\frac{\rm b}{\rm c}\)?
Diciamo: \[\begin{cases}{\rm a}=\cos\left(\beta\right)\cdot{\rm c}+\cos\left(\gamma\right)\cdot{\rm b}\\{\rm b}=\cos\left(\gamma\right)\cdot{\rm a}+\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot{\rm c}\\{\rm c}=\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot{\rm b}+\cos\left(\beta\right)\cdot{\rm a}\end{cases}\Longrightarrow\begin{cases}{\rm b}^2-{\rm a}^2=\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot{\rm c}\cdot{\rm b}-\cos\left(\beta\right)\cdot{\rm c}\cdot{\rm a}\\{\rm c}^2-{\rm b}^2=\cos\left(\beta\right)\cdot{\rm a}\cdot{\rm c}-\cos\left(\gamma\right)\cdot{\rm a}\cdot{\rm b}\\{\rm a}^2-{\rm c}^2=\cos\left(\gamma\right)\cdot{\rm b}\cdot{\rm a}-\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot{\rm b}\cdot{\rm c}\end{cases}\] Cioè: \[\frac{{\rm a}^2}{1-\cos\left(\alpha\right)^2}=\frac{{\rm b}^2}{1-\cos\left(\beta\right)^2}=\frac{{\rm c}^2}{1-\cos\left(\gamma\right)^2}\] Si nota invece che l'area del triangolo si può calcolare come: \[\sin\left(\alpha\right)\cdot{\rm b}\cdot{\rm c}={\rm a}\cdot\sin\left(\beta\right)\cdot{\rm c}={\rm a}\cdot{\rm b}\cdot\sin\left(\gamma\right)\] Da cui si ricava: \[\frac{\rm a}{\sin\left(\alpha\right)}=\frac{\rm b}{\sin\left(\beta\right)}=\frac{\rm c}{\sin\left(\gamma\right)}\] Questa formula è infatti equivalente alla precedente.
La relazione tra gli angoli interni di un triangolo si legge dunque: \[\begin{cases}{\rm a}=\cos\left(\beta\right)\cdot{\rm c}+\cos\left(\gamma\right)\cdot{\rm b}\\{\rm b}=\cos\left(\gamma\right)\cdot{\rm a}+\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot{\rm c}\\{\rm c}=\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot{\rm b}+\cos\left(\beta\right)\cdot{\rm a}\end{cases}\Longrightarrow\begin{cases}{\rm b}^2-\cos\left(\gamma\right)\cdot{\rm a}\cdot{\rm b}={\rm c}^2-\cos\left(\beta\right)\cdot{\rm a}\cdot{\rm c}\\{\rm c}^2-\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot{\rm b}\cdot{\rm c}={\rm a}^2-\cos\left(\gamma\right)\cdot{\rm b}\cdot{\rm a}\\{\rm a}^2-\cos\left(\beta\right)\cdot{\rm c}\cdot{\rm a}={\rm b}^2-\cos\left(\alpha\right)\cdot{\rm c}\cdot{\rm b}\end{cases}\] E si può calcolare:
  • \[\cos\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{{\rm b}^2+{\rm c}^2-{\rm a}^2}{2\cdot{\rm b}\cdot{\rm c}}\]
  • \[\cos\left(\beta\right)=\frac{{\rm c}^2+{\rm a}^2-{\rm b}^2}{2\cdot{\rm c}\cdot{\rm a}}\]
  • \[\cos\left(\gamma\right)=\frac{{\rm a}^2+{\rm b}^2-{\rm c}^2}{2\cdot{\rm a}\cdot{\rm b}}\]

5. l'esponenziale e nepero

Siccome: \[\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm x}^{{\rm k}+1}}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!}\le\sum_{{\rm k}\lt{\rm x}+1}\frac{{\rm x}^{{\rm k}+1}}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!}+\frac{{\rm x}^{{\rm x}+1}}{2\cdot\left({\rm x}+2\right)^{\rm x}}\] Possiamo definire: \[\exp\left({\rm x}\right)=\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\mathbb N}}\frac{{\rm x}^{\rm k}}{{\rm k}!}=\sup_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}}\left(\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm x}^{\rm k}}{{\rm k}!}\right)\]
Per \({\rm x}\ge0\), si può dimostrare \(\dots\) \[\lim_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}=\exp\left({\rm x}\right)\]
\[\begin{split} {\rm m}\ge{\rm n}+{\rm n}\cdot\sqrt[{\rm n}]\frac{{\rm n}-{\rm 1}}{\rm n}\Longrightarrow\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}-1&=\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm n}!}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!\left({\rm n}-{\rm k}-1\right)!}\cdot\left(\frac{\rm x}{\rm n}\right)^{{\rm k}+1}\\ &\le\left(\frac{{\rm n}-1}{\rm n}\right)\cdot\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm x}^{{\rm k}+1}}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!}\\ &\le\left(\frac{{\rm m}-{\rm n}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}\cdot\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm x}^{{\rm k}+1}}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!}\\ &\le\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm n}}\frac{{\rm m}!}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!\left({\rm m}-{\rm k}-1\right)!}\cdot\left(\frac{\rm x}{\rm m}\right)^{{\rm k}+1}\\ &\le\sum_{{\rm k}\in{\rm m}}\frac{{\rm m}!}{\left({\rm k}+1\right)!\left({\rm m}-{\rm k}-1\right)!}\cdot\left(\frac{\rm x}{{\rm m}}\right)^{{\rm k}+1}=\left(\frac{{\rm n}^2+{\rm x}}{\rm m}\right)^{\rm m}-1 \end{split}\] Abbiamo: \[\operatorname{limsup}_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}\le\exp\left({\rm x}\right)\le\operatorname{liminf}_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}\Longrightarrow\lim_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}=\exp\left({\rm x}\right)\] Da cui la tesi.
Considerando che: \[\forall\left({\rm n},{\rm m}\right)\in\left({\mathbb N}\setminus1\right)\times\left({\mathbb N}\setminus1\right)\quad{\rm x}\cdot{\rm m}\ge{\rm n}\Longleftrightarrow\left(\frac{{\rm m}+1}{\rm m}\right)^{{\rm m}\cdot{\rm x}}\le\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}\] Abbiamo allo stesso modo: \[\left(\lim_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+1}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}\right)^{\rm x}=\lim_{{\rm n}\in{\mathbb N}\setminus1}\left(\frac{{\rm n}+{\rm x}}{\rm n}\right)^{\rm n}=\exp\left({\rm x}\right)\]

6. le funzioni iperboliche

Si definiscono: \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\exp\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)-1}{2\cdot\exp\left(\alpha\right)}\qquad\cosh\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\exp\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)+1}{2\cdot\exp\left(\alpha\right)}\] Seguono \(\dots\)
I casi particolari: \[\sinh\left(0\right)=0\qquad\cosh\left(0\right)=1\] Le simmetrie: \[\sinh\left(-{\rm a}\right)=-\sinh\left({\rm a}\right)\qquad\cosh\left(-{\rm a}\right)=\cosh\left({\rm a}\right)\] E pitagora: \[\cosh\left({\rm x}\right)^2-\sinh\left({\rm x}\right)^2=1\]
Seguono le formule di addizione:
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)+\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)=\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)+\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)-\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)=\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)-\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)\]
Seguono \(\dots\)
Le formule di Werner:
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+\sinh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)-\sinh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\]
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)-\cosh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\beta\right)=\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)+\cosh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)}{2}\]
Le formule di prostaferesi:
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)+\sinh\left(\beta\right)=2\cdot\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\sinh\left(\alpha\right)-\sinh\left(\beta\right)=2\cdot\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha\right)-\cosh\left(\beta\right)=2\cdot\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\alpha\right)+\cosh\left(\beta\right)=2\cdot\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha+\beta}{2}\right)\cdot\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha-\beta}{2}\right)\]
Siccome: \[\cosh\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=2\cdot\cosh\left(\alpha\right)^2-1=1+2\cdot\sinh\left(\alpha\right)^2\] Ricaviamo le formule di bisezione: \[\sinh\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\sqrt{\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha\right)-1}{2}}\qquad\cosh\left(\frac{\alpha}{2}\right)=\sqrt{\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha\right)+1}{2}}\]

7. le tangenti iperboliche

Definiamo: \[\tanh\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\sinh\left(\alpha\right)}{\cosh\left(\alpha\right)}\qquad\coth\left(\alpha\right)=\frac{\cosh\left(\alpha\right)}{\sinh\left(\alpha\right)}\] Seguono \(\dots\)
Le simmetrie:
Seguono \(\dots\)
Le formule di addizione per \(\tanh\): \[\begin{split} \tanh\left(\alpha+\beta\right)&=\frac{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)+\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{1+\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)+1}{\coth\left(\beta\right)+\tanh\left(\alpha\right)}\\ &=\frac{1+\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{\coth\left(\alpha\right)+\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\coth\left(\beta\right)+\coth\left(\alpha\right)}{\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)+1} \end{split}\qquad\begin{split} \tanh\left(\alpha-\beta\right)&=\frac{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)-\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{1-\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)-1}{\coth\left(\beta\right)-\tanh\left(\alpha\right)}\\ &=\frac{1-\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{\coth\left(\alpha\right)-\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\coth\left(\beta\right)-\coth\left(\alpha\right)}{\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)-1} \end{split}\] Le formule di addizione per \(\coth\): \[\begin{split} \coth\left(\alpha+\beta\right)&=\frac{1+\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)+\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\coth\left(\beta\right)+\tanh\left(\alpha\right)}{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)+1}\\ &=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)+\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{1+\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)+1}{\coth\left(\beta\right)+\coth\left(\alpha\right)} \end{split}\qquad\begin{split} \coth\left(\alpha-\beta\right)&=\frac{1-\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)-\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\coth\left(\beta\right)-\tanh\left(\alpha\right)}{\tanh\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)-1}\\ &=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)-\tanh\left(\beta\right)}{1-\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\tanh\left(\beta\right)}\\ &=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)\cdot\coth\left(\beta\right)-1}{\coth\left(\beta\right)-\coth\left(\alpha\right)} \end{split}\]
Le formule di duplicazione: \[\tanh\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=\frac{2\cdot\tanh\left(\alpha\right)}{1+\tanh\left(\alpha\right)^2}\qquad\coth\left(2\cdot\alpha\right)=\frac{\coth\left(\alpha\right)^2+1}{2\cdot\coth\left(\alpha\right)}\]
Osservare che:
  • \[\cosh\left(\theta\right)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\tanh\left(\theta\right)^2}}=\frac{1+\tanh\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2}{1-\tanh\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2}\qquad\sinh\left(\theta\right)=\frac{\tanh\left(\theta\right)}{\sqrt{1-\tanh\left(\theta\right)^2}}=\frac{2\cdot\tanh\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)}{1-\tanh\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2}\]
  • \[\cosh\left(\theta\right)=\frac{\coth\left(\theta\right)}{\sqrt{\coth\left(\theta\right)^2-1}}=\frac{\coth\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2+1}{\coth\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2-1}\qquad\sinh\left(\theta\right)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{\coth\left(\theta\right)^2-1}}=\frac{2\cdot\coth\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)}{\coth\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)^2-1}\]
